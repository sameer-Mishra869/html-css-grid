Questions & Answers

1. What does fr stand for?
fr means free space. It divides the available space into equal parts.

2. How many columns are created?
Three columns are created because we used 1fr 1fr 1fr.

3. What happens when screen size is reduced?
The boxes become smaller and adjust inside the container.

4. Do items overflow or adjust?
Items adjust automatically. They do not overflow outside the container.

5. Why is fr better than px for layouts?
fr is flexible and adjusts with screen size, but px is fixed.
